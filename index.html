"Bas Tera Saath Chahat Hoon"

Jo kisi ko kabhi naseeb na hui,

Waisi ek mulaaqat chahta hoon,

Bhagte waqt ko rok kar thodi der,

Tere saath do pal ki baat chahta hoon.

Main tumhara saath chahta hoon…

Tum paas ho — bas itna ehsaas chahta hoon,

Bheed mein bhi sirf tera hi saath chahta hoon.

Apni haathon mein tera haath ho,

Aur us lamhe mein poori kainaat chahta hoon.

Main bas tera saath chahta hoon…

Meri aankhon mein basi rahe teri tasveer,

Har khwaab mein tera hi zikr ho,

Mere kaanon mein goonjti rahe teri payal,

Jaise har saans mein tera hi fikr ho.

Na daulat, na shauhrat, na koi aur khwaahish,

Bas itni si meri har baat chahta hoon —

Har subah teri muskaan se ho shuru,

Aur har raat tera khwaab chahta hoon.

Main bas… tera saath chahta hoon. ❤️✨
